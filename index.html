Whole Body Stretching Exercises 🧘

🪑 Neck Rolls

Slowly roll your neck clockwise, then counter-clockwise. (10 seconds each way)

👐 Shoulder Shrugs

Raise your shoulders to your ears and release. (10 reps)

🙆 Side Bends

Stand tall and reach one hand overhead while bending to the opposite side. (5 reps per side)

🦵 Hamstring Stretch

Sit and reach toward your toes. Hold for 15 seconds.

🧘 Child's Pose

Kneel down and stretch arms forward. Hold for 30 seconds.

Step-by-Step Stretch Routine:

  1. 🪑 Neck Rolls – Slowly roll your neck clockwise, then counter-clockwise. (10 seconds each way)
  2. 👐 Shoulder Shrugs – Raise your shoulders to your ears and release. (10 reps)
  3. 🙆 Side Bends – Stand tall and reach one hand overhead while bending to the opposite side. (5 reps per side)
  4. 🦵 Hamstring Stretch – Sit and reach toward your toes. Hold for 15 seconds.
  5. 🧘 Child's Pose – Kneel down and stretch arms forward. Hold for 30 seconds.
